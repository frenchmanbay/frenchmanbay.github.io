Amenities

All Cottages

Frenchman Bay Cottages would like to make your stay comfortable and relaxing. To help you enjoy your time with us, we offer a clean and fresh atmosphere.

You will find that our cottages offer you all the luxuries of home, air conditioning, dishwasher, central-vac, laundry facilities and wi-fi. The decks are a great place to relax in the sunshine or enjoy a cool breeze beneath the tall maple trees.

Just a short walk takes you to the sandy beach, which is only used by the nearby cottagers. Enjoy the spectacular one-of-a-kind sunsets on your leisurely evening stroll.
